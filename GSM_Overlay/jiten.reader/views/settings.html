Mass Review

Review every word currently visible on screen as "good" in one go. Trigger it with a keybind (set one under Keybinds) or the ✅ button in the status bar. Mastered and blacklisted words are always skipped, as are words you graded or auto-failed earlier in this reading session.

It is not recommended to check "Young" and "Mature" unless you exclusively use Jiten Reader and want to push their next review further out.

When on, the first key press shows a brief "press again" message and the second press starts the review, so you can't trigger it by accident. The status-bar button always shows a confirmation dialog regardless of this setting.

Reviewing the same word again and again in a short span can throw off how the SRS schedules it. To prevent that, the automatic features — mass review and auto-fail — leave a word alone for this many hours after its last review, counting any review (a mass review, an auto-fail, or a grade you gave it yourself). So re-reading a page or flipping back and forth won't keep re-grading the same words, and these features won't override a grade you just gave. Set to 0 to turn this off (not recommended).

If you deliberately open a word's dictionary popup and leave it open past the time below, that word is reviewed as "again" — opening the dictionary usually means you didn't know it. Quick, accidental opens are ignored, and popups that appear automatically on hover never trigger this.

Mining

Mining adds a word to your selected word list as well as the surrounding sentence as a custom example sentence for that word, provided it is no longer than 150 characters. You can store up to 3 example sentences per word; once that quota is reached, mining still adds the word but keeps your existing sentences.

Select a word list deck to mine words into. Word list decks can be created on jiten.moe.

When enabled, the + button mines directly to your selected deck. When disabled, it shows a deck picker each time.

When enabled, reviewing a card also adds it to your selected word list, unless the word is already in that list.

Keybinds
Keybind help

Press escape to clear. Holding modifiers like shift or alt is supported.
Hotkeys are based on key position and independent of your current keyboard layout.
Note: Keybinds currently always work on the word you are hovering your mouse over, even if you have the popup for another word open.
Note: Keybinds consisting of only special keys (Ctrl, Alt, Shift) should work when the window is out of focus, others do not.
Additionally, Left Mouse Click (without modifiers) as well as Right Mouse Click (no matter the modifiers) are not supported.

Anki Integration

To find your AnkiConnect endpoint URL, go to Anki -> Tools -> Add-ons -> AnkiConnect -> Config.

Enter the webBindAddress as well as the webBindPort as webBindAddress:webBindPort - the default is 127.0.0.1:8765.

Install the AnkiConnect add-on if you haven't already.

You can define an additional proxy for the AnkiConnect endpoint. This is useful if you are using asbplayer's auto capture option, for example.

With this, you can tunnel only a select few requests through the proxy to avoid triggering captures on blacklist items, for example.
