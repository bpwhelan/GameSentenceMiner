Changelog

1.3.0 (2026.06.29)
  • Added a distraction-free reader mode accessible through the extension's status bar, the extension's popup or the right-click context menu
  • Added a basic PDF reader accessible through the extension icon
  • Fixed furigana display on Ttsu Reader and Yatsu reader
  • Fixed ASBPlayer parsing
  • Small bug fixes & optimisations
1.2.1 (2026.06.09)
  • Fixed a bug where the mass review keybind was always active, even when not parsing
1.2.0 (2026.06.09)
  • Added the possibility to Mass Review every word currently visible on screen as "good" with a single button in the status bar or a keybind. Configurable in settings > mass review
  • Added an option to auto-fail cards when you open the pop-up (meaning that you forgot to recall), off by default. Configurable in settings > mass review
  • Added an option to auto-mine on review, reviewing a card will add it to your selected word list (unless it's already in it). Configurable in settings > mining
  • The popup now shows the decks and word lists a word belongs to, with a checkmark when it's in one of your word lists. You can toggle it off in settings > popup
  • Words can now be styled differently if they are in a deck, with the option to have different styling if they're in a media deck, a global frequency deck or a word list
  • Simplified custom domain syntax to not require protocol and wildcards
  • Redundant words are now handled with custom styling and not being able to review them
  • You can place the close button at the bottom left of the popup now (mobile)
  • Redesigned the toaster notifications
  • Added an error when trying to mine a word with no mining deck set
  • Fixed word status not being immediately updated when reviewing words marked as frequent
  • Fixed the forget popup behaviour on mobile
  • Fixed native furigana not working on Yatsu Reader
  • Fixed furigana handling in ttsu reader
  • Fixed text area overflow in the settings
1.1.1 (2026.05.15)
  • Added a setting to deactivate the Deck+ button
  • Fix inconsistent parsing on Yatsu reader
1.1.0 (2026.05.07)
  • Added mining to a Jiten SRS deck, mining to preselected deck configurable in settings > mining. This also sets the current sentence as a custom example sentence
  • Added TTS support with 5 voices, configurable in settings > popup
  • Popup can now be resized by dragging the corner and in settings
  • Fixed Mokuro Reader parsing
  • Fixed Aozora Bunko parsing
  • Added support for Yatsu Reader
  • Added support for Nihongo Tracker
  • Slightly changed the styling of the popup buttons
  • Fixed pitch accents on long words overflowing from the popup
  • Various optimisations
